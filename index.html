DEAL SUMMARY

A Stabilized, Cash-Flowing Asset with Built-In Upside

213–215 Halstead Avenue is a fully built-out, single-tenant restaurant building leased to Augustine’s Salumeria LLC under a true triple-net lease through October 2031, with two 5-year renewal options at 3.5% compounded escalation. The property sits directly across from the Mamaroneck Metro-North station on the New Haven Line and on the corner of Mamaroneck Avenue (locally known as “Restaurant Row”).

✓

In-Place Cash Flow

Stabilized NOI of approximately $67,728 on the post-October 2026 contractual rent step. DSCR of 1.30× against the Ponce Bank loan. Property carries its own debt service without sponsor support.

★

Michelin-Recognized Tenant

Augustine’s Salumeria LLC has been in continuous occupancy since June 2022. Michelin Guide listed (2023–present). Operators Marc & Brianne Taxiera opened a successful second concept — Vida Restaurant in Thornwood — in October 2024.

↗

Development Upside

C-2 Central Commercial zoning permits up to two additional stories. Proposed three-story office mixed-use development with rooftop addition is largely as-of-right under current zoning — lower life-safety threshold and reduced cost vs. residential overlay.
